Roadmap

Transforming HR
in Three Phases.

A phased approach to building AI-powered HR infrastructure — from eliminating repetitive tasks to building predictive intelligence.

01

Phase 1

HR Prompt Library

A practical, ready-to-use library of AI prompts built specifically for HR teams. Covers job descriptions, manager conversation scripts, PIPs and corrective actions, and HR process templates. Every prompt is designed with built-in guardrails: no personally identifiable information, compliance-safe language, bias-aware framing, and clear human review checkpoints. The goal is giving HR professionals a toolkit they can trust and use on day one, regardless of which AI tool they're working with.

Active
02

Phase 2

Headcount Intelligence

Turning raw headcount data into instant, decision-ready insights using AI. Instead of spending hours building reports manually, HR teams can analyze spans of control, layers of management, manager-to-IC ratios, headcount trends over time, open headcount vs. budget, and contractor vs. FTE mix. The workflow is simple: export your data, feed it to AI, ask your question in plain language, and get charts, summaries, and recommendations you can drop straight into a leadership presentation. No analyst required.

Up Next
03

Phase 3

People Analytics & Predictive Insights

Using performance reviews, goal data, comp history, and engagement surveys to predict talent outcomes before they happen. Covers attrition risk scoring, promotion readiness, flight risk detection, and talent pool mapping for succession planning. The inputs are data HR already has. The output is actionable intelligence that lets leadership get ahead of problems instead of reacting to them.
